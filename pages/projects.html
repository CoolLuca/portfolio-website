Data Visualisation Research Project

The objective of this project was to investigate the correlation between median hourly wage, median house price, and avocado prices/sales in the United States. A comprehensive data analysis was performed utilizing various analytical methods and practices such as exploratory data analysis, statistical analysis, and regression analysis. The analysis was conducted using the popular data analysis tools, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. Results of the analysis revealed a significant disparity in the growth rates between median house prices and median hourly wages, with house prices increasing at a rate twice that of wages from 1964 to 2020, whereas avocado prices and sales volume were found to have an insignificant effect on a persons ability to purchase a house in comparison to wage growth.
